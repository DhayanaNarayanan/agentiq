DATA ANALYSIS REPORT

Executive Summary:
This report presents an analysis of a large and comprehensive dataset consisting of 284,807 rows and 31 columns. Our analysis provides insights into the key characteristics of the dataset and offers recommendations for improving classification accuracy and model performance.

1. The dataset contains 284,807 rows and 31 columns, with all columns except 'Class' being of type float64.

3. The 'Amount' column has a large range (0 to 25,691.16) and a mean of 88.35, suggesting potential outliers and skewness.

5. The presence of correlated features, skewness, and outliers may require special handling and transformation for effective modeling.
